About

My name is Jayden Bayangos-Brandt, I’m from Melbourne, Australia, and live in the faraway suburb of Cranbourne with my beautiful wife. I have a bachelor’s degree in Information Technology from RMIT. I have been working as an Android Software Engineer for the past 7 years. I have worked on a variety of projects, from small startups to large corporations. I have experience working in various areas of Android development, ranging from Jetpack Compose to Room, PDFs and troublesome 3rd Party SDKs. Always keen to approach a problem with curiosity.

I am passionate about technology and love learning about all kinds of new things.

I have contributed to an open-source project which has always been something on my bucket list!

I’ve been deeply involved in making my home an efficient electric home. Today I pay nothing for my electricity and disconnected from gas in 2023. My home is also super powered with HomeAssistant! One day I hope to document all the steps I took to get to this point.

I love the outdoors and enjoy hiking and camping. I have been to many places around Australia and have seen some amazing sights. I also love combining technology with this hobby of mine and have built up a little bit of an electrical system for my camping setup. Coffee and Toast are never that far for me!

I am very curious about Artificial intelligence and understanding how it works, so far I’ve only scratched the surface by training my own LORA in this blog post. There’s a good chance it is now outdated but at the time it was a great learning experience.

I’m also a massive gamer with some of my favourite games being the Witcher 3, Skyrim, Cyberpunk 2077 and the Mass Effect series. There’s more games I like but I could list those endlessly! I also enjoy playing all kinds of board games. Some favourite pastimes have been Dixit, Carcassonne and Secret Hitler.

I am currently working as a Senior Software Engineer at Bound. I am always looking for new opportunities to work on exciting projects. Feel free to reach out to me if you have any questions or would like to work together.
