ANZ Plus

2019

As part of Itty Bitty Apps, I was the first Android Engineer to work on this greenfield project. Not often you get an opportunity to click “Create Project” in Android Studio. I was deeply involved with many significant aspects of the app, from establishing the architecture, developing the authentication flows, and pioneering roles in the project such as Domain Enablement.

My time here took me to different parts of the app starting in Transactions, Registration and Onboarding, Open Banking and finally involved in the Home Loan journey. The team may have been the largest Native Mobile team I have ever been a part of with a peak count of over 150 iOS and Android Engineers.

I am very proud of the culture that I helped nurture here and everything the team has achieved.

You can find the app on the Google Play Store.
