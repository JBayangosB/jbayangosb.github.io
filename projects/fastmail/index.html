Fastmail

2018

As part of Itty Bitty Apps, I conducted an audit of the Fastmail project. I identified and provided recommendations for a variety of technical tasks with the aim of uplifting the project to meet modern Android standards. After producing this report, I was engaged to implement the recommendations. This involved a complete rewrite of the app to use modern Android architecture components and best practices.

After that successful rewrite, I went on to be involved with even more substantial enhancements to the project, going beyond uplifting to build brand new features such as e2e encrypted notifications and in-app PDF viewing.

This project taught me how to balance clearing technical debt with feature innovation a skill I’ve applied to every project since.

You can find the app on the Google Play Store.
